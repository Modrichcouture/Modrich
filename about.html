About Modrich Couture

Modrich Couture is a premier multi-brand men’s apparel showroom offering a wide range of premium-quality fashion products, including branded shirts, trousers, T-shirts, denims, and innerwear. Designed as a complete fashion destination for men, Modrich Couture caters to customers seeking style, quality, and comfort under one roof.

In addition to renowned brands, Modrich Couture also features its own exclusive collection of fabrics and garments crafted to export-quality standards, ensuring exceptional value and craftsmanship.

Established in 2025, Modrich Couture was founded with a vision to provide superior-quality products and outstanding customer service to fashion-conscious customers across India. The showroom serves both retail and wholesale customers, making premium men’s fashion accessible to a wider audience.

Conveniently located at Joel Plaza, Pallikavala, Kattappana, Modrich Couture continues to redefine men’s fashion by combining quality, variety, and customer satisfaction in every shopping experience.
